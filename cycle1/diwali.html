DIWALI: THE FESTIVAL OF LIGHTS

Diwali, the Festival of Lights, is a major Hindu celebration symbolizing the triumph of light over darkness and good over evil. It marks Lord Rama's return after defeating Ravana, as told in the Ramayana. People celebrate by lighting diyas, making rangoli, bursting fireworks, and exchanging gifts and sweets. Sikhs, Jains, and Buddhists also observe Diwali for their own significant religious events.
