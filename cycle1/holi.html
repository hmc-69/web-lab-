HOLI: A JOYFUL AND COLORFUL FESTIVAL

Holi, the Festival of Colors, is a vibrant Hindu celebration marking spring's arrival, the victory of good over evil, and renewed bonds. People throw colored powders, dance, sing, and feast in joyful unity. Its roots include Prahlad's rescue by Lord Vishnu and the playful love of Krishna and Radha, especially celebrated in Mathura and Vrindavan.
